About Us

IFA Trading PLC is a renowned Ethiopian company specializing in high-quality coffee exports and the import of cutting-edge computer and network devices. With decades of experience, we are committed to delivering excellence in every aspect of our operations.

Ethiopian Coffee Export

Yirgacheffe Coffee

Yirgacheffe is renowned worldwide for its unique flavor profile and fruity, floral aroma. Grown in the high altitudes of the Gedeo Zone, this coffee is celebrated for its bright acidity and sweet, complex taste. It is highly sought after by specialty coffee roasters and coffee lovers alike.

Sidamo Coffee

Sidamo coffee offers a rich body, vibrant acidity, and an earthy, spicy flavor. It is grown in the Sidamo region of Ethiopia, where the unique climate conditions contribute to the production of some of the finest coffees in the world. This coffee is known for its citrusy notes and smooth finish.

Harrar Coffee

Harrar coffee is grown in the eastern highlands of Ethiopia and is one of the oldest coffee varieties in the world. It has a wild, fruity flavor with hints of blueberry and dark chocolate, making it a favorite among coffee connoisseurs. Its full body and intense aroma make it a standout.
